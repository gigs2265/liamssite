But the term used to name Mary is
KecharitomeneFull of Grace
the perfect passive participle of "charitoo." charitoo (verb) comes from the same Greek root of “charis” - which means “grace” and charitoó means to fill or endow with grace or in other: "Hail, one who has been full of grace." Mary was already filled with grace and there is no room for sin in her before the Annunciation, the implication being that she was immaculate.
